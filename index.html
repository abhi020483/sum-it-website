The gap

Small businesses are stuck with software built for accountants.

— every freelancer, every quarter

Receipt chaos

Receipts still live in shoeboxes, glove compartments, and email inboxes.

Manual VAT entry

Typing in dates and VAT amounts by hand is slow and easy to get wrong.

No idea what each job earns

Project profitability is invisible until the work is already done.

Tax reserve uncertainty

The big question — "how much will I owe?" — goes unanswered until the deadline.

Tools built for the wrong person

Pro accounting tools assume you speak accounting. Most small operators don't, and don't want to.

Handoff headaches

Getting clean data to a bookkeeper or Steuerberater still means emailing PDFs back and forth.

The product

Everything a small business needs to stay on top of the money.

Built for the person doing the work, not for accountants. Four modules, one app — receipt in, tax-ready out.

OCR Upload — the heart of Sum-IT

  • Photograph a receipt or upload a PDF — JPG, PDF, HEIC
  • Reads vendor, date, net, VAT, totals & invoice number automatically
  • Detects NL, EN and DE documents on the fly
  • Suggests the right tax code — you confirm before anything is saved

Finances

  • Expenses, invoices and tax reserve in one place
  • Invoices pre-filled with your details and logo; status tracked
  • Overdue reminders sent automatically
  • BTW (NL) and USt (DE) handled correctly, incl. German §14 rules

Dashboard & KPIs

  • Three clear cards — money in, money out, tax reserve
  • Income-vs-expense chart and the week ahead
  • Drag-and-drop widgets on desktop; a clean donut on mobile

Projects

  • A profit & loss view per job — invoices in, costs out
  • Link receipts and invoices to a project in one tap
  • See which engagements actually make money, in real time

Trust & security

Sum-IT handles your financial life, so it's built to be safe with it.

Your data stays in the EU

All data is stored and processed exclusively in AWS EU regions — a hard GDPR requirement, never an afterthought.

GDPR by design

Full compliance across collection, storage and processing. You can request deletion of your personal data at any time.

Audit-safe records

Records can't be silently changed; every correction is timestamped against the original, and source documents are kept — meeting NL retention rules and German GoBD.

Encrypted end to end

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest, with access controls enforced at every layer.
