The product

One suite. Everything a Dutch freelancer actually needs.

  • Vision LLM extracts vendor, date, amount, VAT

Local community activation

Your network earns you money. Ours does too.

Sum-IT isn't just bookkeeping software — it's the payment layer for your local business community. When you pay a subcontractor, hire a local electrician, or settle a job with a partner you found on our Business Map, that transaction runs through Sum-IT's community rails.
